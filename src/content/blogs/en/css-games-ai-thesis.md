My master's thesis focused on the development of an educational video game for learning CSS, with AI-assisted code evaluation.

The prototype combines Godot, web technologies, a Node.js/Express backend, Wallace CSS, and language models to provide contextual feedback through an assistant called **Hemis**.

The project was evaluated with 50 Computer Systems Engineering students. The average score improved from **5.84 in the pretest** to **8.12 in the posttest**, and the SUS usability score reached **81.4 points**.
