Creating a course is not just recording code. It is a design problem: how to organize knowledge so someone else can move forward with clarity.

At EDteam, I was the lead instructor of **Astro desde cero** and **Tu primer portafolio web profesional con Astro**. Both courses reflect an important part of my profile: frontend development, clear communication, and practical education.

Astro is a great tool for teaching because it connects components, performance, content, and real websites without adding unnecessary complexity.
