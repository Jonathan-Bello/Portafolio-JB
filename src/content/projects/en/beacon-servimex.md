Beacon was the first site in what I remember as the Servimex group of projects. It was a landing page focused on medical-grade monitors and commercial presentation.

I built the page from scratch and handled the modal experience, where both a technical sheet and a form had to coexist without making the interaction feel heavy.

This project became the first piece of a small ecosystem of related medical technology brands.
