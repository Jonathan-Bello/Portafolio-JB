Monarca Camellia was a simple landing page for what appears to be a tea-related brand. The site had a strong blue visual direction and a clear brand-oriented structure.

The most practical part was deployment. The client had their own Bluehost server and gave me FTP access, so I uploaded Astro's static build manually. That is one of the reasons I like Astro: the final output can run almost anywhere as plain HTML, CSS and assets.

The main visual challenge was working with logo patterns and making them feel integrated into the design.
