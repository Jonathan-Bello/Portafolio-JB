Empatiko was the larger site in the Servimex ecosystem. It reused lessons from Beacon and Nobilis, but expanded them into a broader product catalog and support structure.

The most complex part was the dropdown menu with images, products and subcategories. The site also included a support section with PDF downloads and internal company documentation.

It represents the consolidation of a visual family of sites under tighter deadlines and with a larger information architecture.
