Clavijero 68 was a real estate landing page for an apartment development in Mexico City. I built it from scratch based on an existing design.

The main technical detail was creating a modal with a Formspree form and making the final visual sections work correctly across the page. Some images had to cross section boundaries, which made the layout more delicate than it first appeared.

It was an agile project, delivered quickly, and later the client moved on. For my portfolio, it remains a clear example of landing page implementation with forms, tracking and visual precision.
