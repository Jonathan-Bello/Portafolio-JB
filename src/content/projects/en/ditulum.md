Ditulum was one of the first larger websites I built completely by myself. It was no longer just a landing page: it included home, contact, services and individual pages for each service.

The project kept part of my early development style, including EDgrid, a library I learned through EDteam before Tailwind CSS became my default. Looking back at it gives me nostalgia because it shows a very specific stage of how I used to build interfaces.

It was also a moment of confidence. I realized I could build a full bilingual website, connect tracking tools and organize a complete visual system from a Figma design.
