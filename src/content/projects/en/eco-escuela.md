Eco Escuela was a landing page for a public speaking school initiative promoted by YGU's director. My role was to build the web presence quickly for marketing purposes.

The site was implemented with Astro, Tailwind CSS and CSS. The most delicate part was the visual work: curved lines, overlapping graphics and a yellow palette that required careful handling to avoid looking harsh.

It represents a quick but visually intentional landing page for a project that was expected to move fast.
