Basmar Beauty was an elegant perfume landing page with catalog behavior, product modals and a video-based visual atmosphere.

The challenge was managing content and interaction density while preserving the refined style of the design. Inside the modal, product information and image carousel behavior had to remain clear.

This project also marked a transition in the design workflow at YGU, after a change in the design team.
