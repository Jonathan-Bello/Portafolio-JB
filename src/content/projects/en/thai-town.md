Thai Town was a small restaurant website built with Astro. It included home, about, contact and menu sections.

The most memorable technical detail was working with curved text that followed a defined SVG path, especially for the main dish visuals. The menu itself was embedded through an iframe to keep the workflow lightweight.

It is a quick, efficient project that now remains as part of the residual record of sites I have built.
