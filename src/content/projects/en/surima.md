Surima was a brief landing page requested with urgency. It was one of the first projects where I experimented with Astro 5 while keeping the scope small enough to move quickly.

The page was built from scratch with Astro and Tailwind CSS. The most memorable visual element was the carousel, which gave the site enough motion and personality without adding unnecessary complexity.

For my portfolio, Surima represents speed: a small site delivered in a few days with a clean structure and a practical implementation.
