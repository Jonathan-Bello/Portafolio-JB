Arctic Roots is one of the most beautiful websites I have built. It presents travel experiences in Iceland and Greenland, with packages, destinations and a strong sense of place.

It was also the first large site I built after a change in the design workflow. The new designs only came mostly in desktop form, so I had to make more responsive decisions myself while preserving the intended visual rhythm.

For my portfolio, Arctic Roots represents maturity in Astro, multilingual structure and visual adaptation under a more demanding design style.
