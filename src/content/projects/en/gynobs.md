Gynobs was a straightforward medical website built with Astro. The design was already provided and my role was to translate it into a clean, responsive implementation.

The doctors page was one of the most detailed sections because it required a particular grid layout and different arrangements for each profile. The challenge was to keep the page minimal while still respecting all the information the design required.

It represents a sober type of frontend work: clear, professional and focused on trust.
