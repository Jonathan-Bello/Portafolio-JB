Volta Capital is a bilingual corporate site for a group of energy-related companies. It follows the newer YGU design and development standards, with a clean structure and reusable sections.

There were no unusually difficult technical problems, but the project shows a mature workflow: Astro, Tailwind CSS, some React pieces and better automation habits supported by AI during development.

It represents a recent stage of my professional work, where building this kind of corporate site became faster, more organized and more predictable.
