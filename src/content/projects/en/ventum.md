Ventum is an old inherited YGU project. I did not build the site and my participation was limited, but it remains in the portfolio because it represents another kind of professional responsibility.

My work was mainly to migrate the project away from a former personal GitHub account, move it into a more controlled company structure and update contact details such as a WhatsApp number.

It is not a project of visual authorship, but of maintenance, ownership cleanup and operational continuity.
