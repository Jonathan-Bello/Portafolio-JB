May Pro is a construction product website focused on mortars, stuccos, adhesives and technical resources. I built it from scratch using the Astro workflow I had already refined in previous catalog sites.

The project follows a structure similar to Tradeco: product categories, descriptions, downloadable technical sheets and transitions between product pages. The visual challenge came from design lines and overlapping elements that had to cross sections without breaking layout.

It represents experience in technical product catalogs, where clarity, downloadable resources and performance matter as much as visual presentation.
