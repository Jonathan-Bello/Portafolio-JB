Worldpark was a commercial site for industrial developments. I joined a project that already had a base, but I was assigned the heavy part: organizing warehouses, locations, technical sheets, addresses, areas, PDFs and media.

One of the biggest practical challenges was video handling. The uploaded files were heavy, so I used previous experience with Cloudinary to reduce delivery weight while keeping acceptable visual quality.

For my portfolio, Worldpark represents work with real data volume: catalogs, technical resources, videos and structured content that had to remain clear for a commercial audience.
