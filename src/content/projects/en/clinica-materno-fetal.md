Clinica Materno Fetal was a medical website built under the newer YGU design standards. It was a straightforward project, but with enough structure to be more than a simple landing page.

The services section was the most important part, because it worked as a catalog that guided users toward booking through WhatsApp. The client preferred that contact flow, so the site focused on presenting information clearly rather than collecting sensitive data.

It represents a clean, reusable Astro implementation for a medical context.
