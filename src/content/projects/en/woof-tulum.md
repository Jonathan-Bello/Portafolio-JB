Woof Tulum was a small landing page that I joined after it had already been started. My work focused on the bilingual experience, creating mirrored Spanish and English sections and making the language switch feel clean.

To avoid scroll and state issues, the language change returned the user smoothly to the top of the page. It was a simple solution, but effective for the size and needs of the site.

This project represents early maintenance work: understanding an existing Gatsby structure, adding internationalization behavior and keeping the result simple, usable and aligned with the original page.
