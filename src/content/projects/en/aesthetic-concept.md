Aesthetic Concept is a clean catalog site for chairs and furniture products. I built it from scratch with Astro, focusing on image presentation, minimal luxury and smooth gallery behavior.

The most interesting piece was the gallery, where I used what I had learned from View Transitions to create a floating navigation style that later influenced other projects.

It is a small but polished project, useful for showing visual restraint and product-focused frontend work.
