Nobilis was built as a sister page to Beacon. It reused a similar structure and visual system, changing the focus toward medical servers and related products.

The value of the project was efficiency: adapting a proven base, changing brand details and keeping the experience consistent across related sites.

For my portfolio, Nobilis represents practical reuse, consistency and speed in commercial frontend work.
