Tycoon was mostly a maintenance project. The site already existed and my work centered on keeping the corporate brand catalog updated.

The difficult part was not a complex integration, but the manual nature of the work. Brands changed by country or region, assets had to be updated, and the site was not as componentized as I would have liked.

It represents a very real kind of professional work: maintaining inherited pages, understanding their structure and making precise updates without overengineering the task.
